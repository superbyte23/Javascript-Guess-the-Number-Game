Javascript Number Guess Game

Welcome to this engaging JavaScript Number Guess Game! Follow the instructions below and test your luck.

  1. Game Objective:
    • Your mission is to guess the correct number between 1 and 100.
  2. How to Play:
    • Enter your guess when prompted, keeping in mind the valid range of 1-100.
  3. Feedback System:
    • The program will provide instant feedback through alert messages, guiding you on whether your guess is greater or lesser than the mystery number.
  4. Guess Limit:
    • You have a total of 10 guesses to crack the code. Use them wisely!
  5. Winning Moment:
    • If you manage to guess the correct number, congratulations! The game will celebrate your victory, and the challenge concludes.

Note!!! Stay sharp, as you only have 10 chances to unveil the mystery. Good luck, and may the odds be in your favor!
